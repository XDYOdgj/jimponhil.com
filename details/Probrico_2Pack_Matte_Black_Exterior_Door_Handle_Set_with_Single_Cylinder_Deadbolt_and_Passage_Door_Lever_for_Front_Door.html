Probrico 2Pack Matte Black Door Handle Set

Upgrade your home’s security and style with the Probrico 2Pack Matte Black door handle set. Designed to blend functionality with elegance, this set offers sleek, matte black finishes that complement a wide range of interior and exterior designs. The unique key-alike system ensures that all handles can be operated with a single key, making it easier than ever to manage access.

Each handle includes a single cylinder deadbolt with a thumb-turn mechanism, allowing you to lock and unlock from both sides with ease. The adjustable backset feature ensures a secure and customizable fit for doors of different thicknesses. Installation is simple, requiring only a Phillips screwdriver—perfect for DIY enthusiasts or those looking to upgrade their front or back doors quickly.

With ANSI Grade 3 certification, these handles are built for long-term durability, capable of withstanding over 200,000 cycles of use without compromising performance.
