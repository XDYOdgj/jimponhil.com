Crystmetal Privacy Indicator Lever Lock

The Crystmetal Privacy Indicator Lever Lock is designed to enhance security and reduce hesitation in shared spaces. Its clear visibility feature helps users quickly identify if a room is occupied or vacant, promoting safety and efficiency. Whether you're in a bathroom, office, or healthcare facility, this lock offers peace of mind with its reliable performance.

Key Features

  • 50-foot visibility indicator window displays 'Occupied' (Red) or 'Vacant' (Green)
  • Stainless steel construction with a matte black finish for easy cleaning and durability
  • Meets ANSI Grade 2 standard for long-term performance and security
  • Simple drop-in installation for standard doors up to 2" thick
  • Reversible handles for left or right-handed users
  • Secure exterior lever with a notch for remote operation using a screwdriver or coin

Why Choose This Lock?

This lock is ideal for both commercial and residential environments, helping to maintain order, safety, and ease of use. Its sleek design blends seamlessly with your space while delivering robust functionality. The Crystmetal Privacy Indicator Lever Lock is a reliable choice for anyone looking to improve privacy and security in their facility.
