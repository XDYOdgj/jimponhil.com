Schlage B60 N 716 Deadbolt

The Schlage B60 N 716 Deadbolt is a powerful addition to your home security system. With a 1-inch hardened steel shaft and anti-drilling and anti-picking pins, it's engineered to provide exceptional resistance against forced entry. This deadbolt not only ensures safety but also blends seamlessly with your home's aesthetic, featuring a timeless Aged Bronze finish that elevates the look of your locks.

Key Features

  • Premium Security: 1-inch hardened steel deadbolt with anti-drill and anti-pick pins, designed to protect against prying and break-in attempts.
  • Easy Installation: Self-aligning screw holes make setup simple without the need for tools, saving you time and hassle.
  • Versatile Use: Perfect for front doors, entryways, and other high-security door applications. It fits both new and existing door systems.
  • Classy Finish: The Aged Bronze color gives your home a refined and elegant look while maintaining durability and style.
  • Integrated Reinforcement: Includes a strike reinforcer with 3-inch screws, providing added protection against door kicks and forced entry.
  • Universal Fit: Designed to work with double-bore doors and standard door sizes, available in both left and right-handed configurations.
  • BHMA Grade 1/AAA Certification: Certified for high security performance, giving you peace of mind knowing your home is well-protected.
  • Professional Build: Built to last with a premium finish that ensures both long-term use and a polished appearance.
