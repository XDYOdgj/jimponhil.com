AkaGear DS10 Pro Keyless Entry Deadbolt: Smart, Secure, and Simple

Elevate your home security with the DS10 Pro Keyless Entry Deadbolt. This modern smart lock combines convenience and safety, allowing you to unlock your front door with a biometric fingerprint, PIN code, mechanical key, or your smartphone. It's designed to adapt to your lifestyle, whether you're at home or away.

With advanced fingerprint recognition technology, the DS10 Pro unlocks your door in under a second, providing fast access for your family members, including children, elderly parents, and guests. It supports up to 50 fingerprint enrollments, making it perfect for households that value both security and ease of use.

Take control of your home's access with the TUYA or Smart Life app. Customize user profiles, set temporary access codes for visitors, and enable automatic locking for added convenience. The one-touch locking with Bluetooth and mute mode ensures smooth late-night entries without unwanted noise.

Powering this smart deadbolt is a long-lasting battery system. With 4 AA batteries, you can expect up to a year of reliable performance. A rechargeable backup ensures you'll never be locked out, even if the batteries run low.

Why Choose the DS10 Pro?

  • Fast and secure fingerprint unlock with 0.3-second recognition speed
  • Smart app access with remote control and access logs
  • Auto-locking feature with customizable delay from 5 to 59 seconds
  • One-touch lock with Bluetooth and mute mode for a quiet home environment
  • Long battery life and rechargeable backup for worry-free operation

The Perfect Blend of Technology and Tradition

Whether you're a tech-savvy homeowner or someone who prefers a reliable backup, the DS10 Pro smart deadbolt is built to meet your needs. Its modern features align with traditional security, making it an ideal solution for the contemporary home.

Don't settle for outdated entry methods. The DS10 Pro offers a smart, secure, and user-friendly alternative that keeps your home safe while making life easier for everyone in it.
