Kwikset SmartCode 270 Venetian Bronze Deadbolt

This smart deadbolt brings convenience and security to your door with its modern design and advanced features. Perfect for anyone who values ease of use and protection, the Kwikset SmartCode 270 offers a reliable, style-conscious solution for your home.

With a keyless entry system, you can access your home effortlessly using a secure electronic touchpad. No more hunting for keys, and no more worrying about losing them. The sleek Venetian Bronze finish is designed to match traditional and elegant door styles, making it a great addition to any home.

Thanks to its auto-lock feature, the deadbolt can be programmed to lock automatically after a customizable delay, giving you peace of mind when you're away. Enjoy up to 12 months of battery life using just 4 AA batteries, and take control of up to 50 user codes for seamless access management. This makes it ideal for families, guests, and even pet owners who need temporary access.

Key Features Include:

  • Secure keyless entry with electronic touchpad
  • Grade 2 BHMA certified for enhanced security and durability
  • Auto-lock function with customizable delay settings
  • Up to 12 months of battery power with 4 AA batteries
  • Customizable user codes for easy access control
