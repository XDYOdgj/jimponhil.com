Probrico Contemporary Dummy Door Handle

Elevate your door's style with this sleek and modern dummy door handle. Designed for doors that only require a pull, it delivers seamless functionality without the need for a traditional latch.

Crafted from high-quality zinc alloy, this handle ensures durability and resilience against everyday wear. Its unique matte nickel finish adds an elegant touch, blending effortlessly with contemporary interiors and exteriors.

With adjustable latch settings suitable for door thicknesses ranging from 35 to 45mm, it offers a customizable fit for any doorway. The built-in backset of 60mm or 70mm provides flexibility, making it ideal for both residential and commercial use.

Installation is a breeze with detailed, easy-to-follow instructions. This handle requires minimal parts, saving you time and effort during the setup process. A perfect solution for anyone seeking both style and simplicity in their door hardware.

Whether you're updating your home or enhancing a commercial space, the Probrico dummy door handle is a reliable and stylish choice. It's not just an accessory—it's an upgrade that adds value to your property.

  • Matte nickel finish for a modern, sleek look

  • Easy installation with clear instructions, minimal parts needed
