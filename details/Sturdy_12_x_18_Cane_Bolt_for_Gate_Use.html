National Hardware Cane Bolt

Key Features

  • Robust yet lightweight construction for both vertical and horizontal applications.

This Cane Bolt is built for durability and ease of use. Whether you're installing a gate or securing a door, its sturdy design ensures peace of mind. Ideal For
